Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential conferences in the technology industry, attracting thousands of innovators, developers, and entrepreneurs from around the globe.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster collaboration, inspire innovation, and empower the next generation of tech leaders. We believe in creating a space where ideas flourish, diversity is celebrated, and the future is built together.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Fields

Renowned AI researcher and founder of FutureTech Labs, Dr. Fields delivered a keynote on ethical AI development in 2022. Her insights continue to shape modern AI frameworks.

James Okoro

Cybersecurity expert and author, James Okoro, spoke in 2021 about defending critical infrastructure in a digital age. His work has influenced major security protocols worldwide.

Lina Chen

Entrepreneur and CEO of GreenCircuit, Lina discussed sustainable tech manufacturing and innovations in green computing during her 2023 session.
